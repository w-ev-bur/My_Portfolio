Adobe Illustrator

Delivery Company Logos

For this project using Adobe Illustrator, I designed these logo variations for a technology forward delivery business. The logo suite includes logos for all use cases such as signage, documentation, websites, apps and more.
The main idea for this project was to highlight how one company could have multiple versions and variations of their logo so they can add it anywhere.

Photography Freelancer Logos

These different logos are drafts i made for a freelance photographer who wanted help building a brand. We came to a decision on the colours and shades to use that worked well together, then it came down to designing the different styles.
I had a handful of ideas that we decided to expand on and interpolate into different forms. I learned a lot from the creation of the designs and was able to work on taking one design and changing it around to create so many more from it.

Creative Studio Logos

These are four different colour schemes made for a creative media and entertainment studio. The lack of clear form or structure in the logo is to highlight the creativity and ability of the business to break out of the traditional mold most entertainment companies follow.
The different colour schemes all appeal to different emotions and feelings. Creating new perspectives even on the same logo.

Menu Colour Variations

These are variations of possible colours that could be used for these menu designs. The colours all have their own positives and some work better than others, but they all work and play to different strengths.
As for the oveall design layout of the menus, I love the way they came out and how I managed to fit all of the items onto them.
